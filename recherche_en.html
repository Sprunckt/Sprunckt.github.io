Research Theme


My research focuses on resolving diverse inverse problems. The objective of my thesis was to retrieve the geometric parameters of a room using acoustic measurements, particularly room impulse responses (RIR).

The approaches I used relied on super-resolution techniques as well as shape optimization.

Keywords: Inverse problems, signal processing, super-resolution, shape optimization.
